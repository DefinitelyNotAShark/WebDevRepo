About

Kakatte Koi Yo! is a fast-paced platform fighter where you and up to three friends battle it out to see who can collect fish faster. Of course, if you want to detonate their chances, you could always go for the bomb in the center.
Jump, swipe, and slap your way to victory in 1v1 or 2v2 local multiplayer battles.
