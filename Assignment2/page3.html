Down By the River

You wander down by the river.
There are many cool rocks down there. You try to collect as many as you can.

All the rocks are very heavy, but you keep collecting them.
You can't bring yourself to put any of them down. They're so heavy.

The rocks are so heavy that you can't walk anymore.
You don't know if you'll ever make it to town, but you have a bunch of sweet rocks now.
Everything is fine.

The End
